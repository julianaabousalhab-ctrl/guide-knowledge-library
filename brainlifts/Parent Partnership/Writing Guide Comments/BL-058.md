# Write in Reality → Lever → Next Step Order

## Pattern

Write and deliver Guide feedback in three distinct moves:

Reality: State what is currently happening using data, frequency, observable behavior, or a student quote.
Lever: Identify the strategy, structure, motivation, or missing Guide action influencing the result.
Next Step: State the specific action the Guide will take to move the student forward.

This order prevents comments from becoming a collection of observations. It gives the student and parent a clear account of where the student stands, why the outcome is occurring, and what the Guide will do next. 

## Evidence

*1- The Guide identifies inconsistent performance, diagnoses the missing structure, and defines a measurable intervention.*

>“I have not yet built a reliable structure for [Student] to reach 2X in any subject. He meets minimums at times but does not sustain daily effort to push beyond, with writing being the most persistent gap. Focused mornings have occasionally helped, but have not translated into full-week output. I have not yet turned his social motivation and growing independence into an academic system that works. His openness to feedback, maturity in accountability, and willingness to take on independent goals are clear wins. The future goal is to implement a concrete, student-owned plan: use a simple daily XP tracker we build together, review progress every Monday/Wednesday, and require one measurable academic push each week so his social energy begins fueling steady academic results.”
